How to Brew Camano Island Coffee for Maximum Flavor

To get the best experience, follow these brewing recommendations:

1. Use Fresh Filtered Water

Water quality directly affects coffee taste. Avoid tap water with heavy chlorine or mineral imbalance.

2. Grind Just Before Brewing

Whole beans retain aroma oils far better than pre-ground coffee.

3. Ideal Brewing Methods

  • Pour-over (best for flavor clarity)
  • French press (best for body and richness)
  • Espresso (best for intensity)

4. Correct Coffee-to-Water Ratio

Use approximately 1:16 ratio (1g coffee per 16g water).
