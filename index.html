How to Brew Your Perfect Cup

   
     
1. Choose Your Coffee
     
        Select your preferred roast—dark, medium, or light. Freshly ground beans give the richest flavor.      
   
   
     
2. Measure the Coffee
     
        Use 1-2 tablespoons of coffee per 6 oz of water, depending on your strength preference.      
   
   
     
3. Boil Water
     
        Heat water to around 200°F (just below boiling) for optimal extraction.      
   
   
     
4. Brew
     
        Pour hot water over your coffee grounds and let it steep for 4-5 minutes, or use your preferred method.      
   
   
     
5. Enjoy
     
        Pour your coffee into a favorite mug, savor the aroma, and enjoy a perfect cup of Camano Island Coffee.
